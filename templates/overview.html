KEY FEATURES

LARO offers a comprehensive set of tools designed to enhance your basketball experience. From finding games to tracking your progress, we've got you covered.

Quick Join

Find and join basketball games happening right now near you. No more searching for pickup games - see who's playing and join instantly.

Create Games

Organize your own basketball games and invite players. Set the time, location, and game type, then share with friends or open to the community.

Track Stats

Keep track of your performance and game history. Monitor your progress, see your strengths, and identify areas for improvement.

Team Building

Create or join teams for regular play and tournaments. Build your squad, compete together, and climb the LARO rankings.
